How to eat more vegetables

1. Keep a veg log

What we measure, we improve - James Clear

2. Buy more vegetables

Might seem obvious, but to eat more veg, you have to buy more veg. Next time you are buying groceries, get at least one vegetable you don't normally buy. Don't worry if you've never had it before, you will figure what to do with it when you get it home.

3. Keep your veggies where you can see them

Is your veg hiding in the drawer at the bottom of the fridge or at the back of a cupboard? Go to the kitchen now and move all the veg to the front of your shelves, as close as you can to eye level. Any veg that doesn't need to be cold or dark can be kept out in full view.

4. Sneak extra veg into your favorite meals

Every time you make a sandwich, add a slice of cucumber, tomato or a letuce leaf. Every time you make bolognese, grate in some carrot or courgette. For each of your go-to meals are, think of an extra vegetable you can add.

5. Start at breakfast
